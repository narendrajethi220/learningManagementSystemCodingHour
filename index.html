Frequently Asked Question

How do I know the right courses for me?

Consider your interests, strengths, and career goals. Research and compare course offerings and seek advice from our Mentors or academic advisors.

How do I purchase a course?

You can purchase a course by visiting the course page and clicking the "Buy Now" or "Add to Cart" button. You can also use a coupon code if you have one.

Are courses refundable?

We offers a 30-day money-back guarantee for most courses. If you're not satisfied with a course, you can request a refund within 30 days of purchasing the course.

How long do courses last?

Our courses are self-paced, so the length of time it takes to complete a course will vary depending on the student. Most courses are offer lifetime access, so students can complete the course at their own pace.

How do I access my purchased courses?

You can access your purchased courses by logging into your account and going to the "My Courses" page.

How can I see my Payment status?

You can see your payment status by visited Payment status link present on the navigation bar, after logging to your account and giving course Id.

Can I share my courses with others?

No, Our courses are for personal use only and cannot be shared with others. Each course purchase allows only one person to access the course.

Are courses updated?

We encourages instructors to update their courses regularly to ensure the content is current and relevant. However, it's ultimately up to the instructor to update the course.
